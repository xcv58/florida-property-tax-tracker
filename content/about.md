# About

Florida Property Tax Tracker is a neutral civic tracking project for Florida's 2026 property tax elimination proposals and materially related reforms.

The project is not affiliated with the State of Florida, the Florida Legislature, any county government, or any advocacy organization. Its purpose is to help readers quickly understand current status, source documents, timelines, and uncertainty.

The tracker does not endorse or oppose any proposal. It separates official records from stakeholder positions and keeps historical status visible when proposals change.

The overview uses color only for procedural status: green for completed steps, yellow for watching or uncertain steps, red for blocked or dead steps, and gray for checkpoints that have not been reached. The monthly runway shows source-backed posture as of the last check; later months are checkpoints to watch, not predictions.

Visitors can use the header controls to switch between system, light, and dark theme modes, or between English and Spanish interface text.
