# Correction Policy

Corrections and source suggestions are welcome.

A correction request should include:

- The claim being challenged.
- A source URL or document.
- The requested correction.
- Whether the source is official, stakeholder, news, analysis, or other.
- The date of the source.

Changes should not be merged if they add unsourced claims, use advocacy language in factual sections, remove historical context without explanation, change proposal status without official support, mix proposal versions without labeling them, or use a news article as the only source for official bill status when an official source exists.
